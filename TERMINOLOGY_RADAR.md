## Narrative shifts

| Previous framing | Better framing | Why | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| One portable kernel implementation | Stable kernel intent plus backend-specific lowerings and measured contracts | Accelerators differ in programming model, memory hierarchy, compiler path, and optimal schedules | [PyTorch](https://pytorch.org/blog/helion-on-tpu-towards-hardware-heterogeneous-kernel-authoring/) |
| Autotuning as a local experiment | Autotuning as a versioned deployment artifact | Selected implementations are conditional on shapes, dtypes, hardware, compiler versions, and search settings | [PyTorch](https://pytorch.org/blog/helion-on-tpu-towards-hardware-heterogeneous-kernel-authoring/) |
| Agent as model plus tools | Agent as a governed workflow service with explicit lifecycle controls | Production reliability depends on scope, policy, actions, evaluation, escalation, telemetry, and controlled change | [OpenAI Presence](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-presence/) |
| Active seats as adoption | Behavioral adoption cohorts plus quality-adjusted outcomes | Activity depth is useful but not equivalent to causal productivity or business value | [GitHub impact dashboard](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-07-22-new-copilot-usage-metrics-impact-dashboard/) |
