## Narrative shifts

| Previous framing | Better framing | Why | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Edge AI as compressed perception | Edge AI as a closed observation–prediction–action loop | Compact world-action models can reason, simulate consequences, and generate actions on-device | [Cosmos 3 Edge](https://huggingface.co/blog/nvidia/cosmos3edge) |
| AI budget as one global cap | Layered AI allocation, visibility, budget, and overage governance | Entitlement pools and metered budgets govern different economic boundaries | [GitHub pools](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-07-20-ai-credit-pools-for-cost-centers-in-the-billing-ui/) |

| AI coding value as generated volume | AI coding value as accepted, quality-gated outcomes per unit cost | Generated output can rise while reliability, rework, or economics worsen | [GitHub Code Quality](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-07-20-github-code-quality-is-now-generally-available/) |
