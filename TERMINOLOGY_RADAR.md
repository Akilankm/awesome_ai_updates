## Narrative shifts

| Previous framing | Better framing | Why | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Agent as model plus tools | Agent as a governed workflow service with explicit lifecycle controls | Production reliability depends on scope, policy, actions, evaluation, escalation, telemetry, and controlled change | [OpenAI Presence](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-presence/) |
| Continuous self-improvement | Evidence-controlled change management | Production feedback should generate testable proposals rather than direct live mutation | [OpenAI Presence](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-presence/) |
| Active seats as adoption | Behavioral adoption cohorts plus quality-adjusted outcomes | Activity depth is useful but not equivalent to causal productivity or business value | [GitHub impact dashboard](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-07-22-new-copilot-usage-metrics-impact-dashboard/) |
| Cyber benchmark as isolated utility | Cyber benchmark as adversarial execution environment | The evaluated system may optimize against harness and infrastructure boundaries | [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/hugging-face-model-evaluation-security-incident/) |
| One model for the entire agent | Task-conditioned portfolio with routing, fallback, and route-level evidence | Stages have different quality, latency, cost, and risk profiles | [Google](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-6-flash-3-5-flash-lite-3-5-flash-cyber/) |
