| Previous assumption | Updated mental model | Consequence | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI coding-tool governance is mainly seat management and aggregate usage | AI engineering governance is repository-scoped and outcome-linked: each repository has different criticality, review economics, security exposure, and automation readiness | Measure AI activity per repository and normalize it against quality, delivery, risk, and cost outcomes | [GitHub repository metrics](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-07-17-repository-level-github-copilot-usage-metrics-generally-available/) |
